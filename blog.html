What is Terrace Farming ?

This type of farming is done on the roof of the house. It can be also called as a roof garden. It is a garden in which vegetables and fruits are grown on terraces, balconies or roofs of buildings. The primary advantage of terraces is improved soil erosion control. It doesn’t matter how big or small your rooftop is, with the right planning and little efforts you can transform your terrace into a farm.

A Quick Guide To Terrace Farming

1. Select the plants

If you want to grow vegetables then start with the ones that grow fast and doesn’t require much effort, like coriander, fenugreek, chilli, capsicum, etc.

Getting Started

First of all clean your terrace. Now choose containers, earthen pots, grow bags and make the pots well drained by putting a hole on the bottom. Make sure the pot should be at least 24inch in height. Now let's prepare the soil. For that we need for collect garden soil, fertilizers, sand and cocopeat(Optional). Mix the items listed above items:- 20% Sand, 20% Cocopeat, 10% Fertilizer, 50% Soil. The soil mixed with the items in called "Garden Mix". Now take the processed soil and its time to put it in the container or pot. Make sure the soil must be well drained. Now the soil is ready for plantation.
